Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2025

Section 1

Introduction

AethericArts ("we," "us," or "our") operates Skydew Islands, a VR multiplayer game available on the Meta Quest platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

This policy is designed to comply with Meta's Voluntary Review Criteria (VRC.Privacy.1 – VRC.Privacy.4) and applicable privacy laws. By using Skydew Islands, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Section 2  ·  VRC.Privacy.1

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account & Profile Information

When you create a profile within Skydew Islands, we may collect:

  • In-game username or display name
  • Avatar customization choices and appearance settings
  • Player preferences and settings saved within the game

Gameplay Data

To provide core game functionality, we may collect:

  • In-game actions, interactions, and progress
  • Multiplayer session data (e.g., server connection details needed for gameplay)
  • Technical logs necessary for bug fixing and performance optimization

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Real name, email address, or contact information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Location data
  • Biometric data (including hand tracking geometry or eye tracking data)
  • Photos, videos, or voice recordings
  • Data from children under 13

Section 3  ·  VRC.Privacy.2

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Operate and maintain the Skydew Islands game experience
  • Enable multiplayer functionality and synchronize game state between players
  • Save your in-game progress, settings, and avatar customizations
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve game performance
  • Ensure compliance with our Terms of Service

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or any purposes unrelated to operating the game.

Section 4  ·  VRC.Privacy.3

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own purposes.

Your information may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other players in the same multiplayer session, to the extent necessary for gameplay (e.g., your in-game username and avatar appearance)
  • If required by law, court order, or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of AethericArts or our users

We do not integrate with third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers.

Section 5  ·  VRC.Privacy.4

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your in-game profile and settings for as long as you use Skydew Islands, or until you request deletion.

You may request deletion of your data by:

  • Using the in-game account or profile deletion option (if available)
  • Contacting us directly at the address listed in Section 7 below

Upon a valid deletion request, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Section 6

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Section 7

Children's Privacy

Skydew Islands is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.
